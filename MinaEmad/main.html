Saint Mary Church

Church of the Virgin Mary in Zeitoun - Cairo

The Church of the Virgin Mary in Zeitoun is considered one of the most famous Coptic Orthodox churches in Egypt. It is associated with the apparitions of the Virgin Mary that occurred there between 1968 and 1971.


General Information

Location:Zeitoun District, Cairo

Date of Construction:1925, officially opened in 1929 by businessman Tawadros Pasha

Apparitions:On April 2, 1968, a series of spiritual apparitions of the Virgin Mary began on the church's domes.

Official Recognition:The Orthodox and Catholic Churches have recognized the authenticity of the apparitions.

The church's design is inspired by the Hagia Sophia and is distinguished by its large domes, making it visible from afar.
